## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account creation
- Basic business details ready (name, niche, target market)
- Access to a web browser (Chrome recommended for best experience)

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card required for signup. Upgrade to premium services after your free consultation.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your MakeItEarn account in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://app.makeitearn.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and create a strong password. Agree to the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@makeitearn.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Add your business name and e-commerce goals (Shopify store, Amazon seller, etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Book Your Initial Consultation

Choose your preferred booking method. Consultations are free and last 30 minutes.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Calendar" icon="calendar">
    On your dashboard at `https://dashboard.makeitearn.com/consultations`, select a time slot.
    
    Available slots update in real-time based on expert availability.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Request" icon="mail">
    Send details to `consult@makeitearn.com` with subject "Free Consultation Request".
    
    Include your timezone and preferred dates.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Phone" icon="phone">
    Call `+1-800-MAKEIT-EARN` (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST).
    
    Have your account email ready for quick verification.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete the Business Assessment

During or after consultation, fill out the assessment to personalize your plan.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Assessment" icon="clipboard-list">
    From dashboard, click "Start Assessment".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer Questions" icon="help-circle">
    Cover your experience level, budget (`<50k`, `50k-200k`, `>200k`), and platforms (Shopify, Amazon, Kickstarter).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results" icon="check-circle">
    Get a customized roadmap emailed within 1 hour.
  </Step>
</Steps>
