---
title: Cannot Connect Shopify Store
description: Fix connection issues with Shopify and MakeItEarn.
---

## Issue

- Unable to link Shopify store to MakeItEarn dashboard
- Error: "Shopify authentication failed"

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    ````
    Ensure your Shopify admin credentials are correct.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable app permissions">
    ````
    In Shopify, go to "Apps" and ensure MakeItEarn access is active.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try again">
    ````
    Reconnect from the MakeItEarn dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
