---

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Shopify account">
    ````
    Visit shopify.com and sign up for a free trial.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect MakeItEarn">
    ````
    In the MakeItEarn dashboard, click "Connect new store" and follow prompts.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch your site">
    ````
    Add products, set up payments, and publish your Shopify store.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
